Live preview

Check a preview of your animation in real time as you draw. Adjust the frame delay on the fly.

Google Sign in

No need to remember yet another password, just use your Google account to sign in.

Export to GIF, PNG...

Several export modes supported. Animated GIFs for sharing, spritesheet PNG/ZIP for bigger projects etc…

Open Source

All the code is open-source and available on GitHub.
http://github.com/juliandescottes/piskel

Private gallery

You can chose to make any of your sprites public or private. Private sprites are only visible to you.

Offline versions

Free desktop & offline applications for Windows, OSX and Linux. Checkout the download page.
